## Autonomous Rapid Transit Feasibility in Toronto
**Course:** INFT-3103 Emerging Technologies — Durham College  
**Date:** December 2025  
**Team:** Lucas Delvoie, Riyaan Hassan, Abdul Hadi, Subeet Singh  

Research analyzing Autonomous Rapid Transit (ART) as a solution for the GTHA's 
transit "missing middle" — medium-distance corridors (15–30 km) underserved by 
local buses but lacking density for subway investment.

**My contributions:** Abstract, Introduction, Technology Overview, Future Outlook

### Key Findings
- ART deploys at ~1/10th the infrastructure cost of LRT ($10–40M/km vs $1.6B/km)
- Jane Street corridor (48,000–55,000 daily riders) identified as optimal pilot
- 2–3 year deployment timeline vs 10–15 years for conventional rail
- Projected 20–30 minute commute reductions for corridor residents
